Our Story

Where Community Meets
Curated Treasures

Small Towne Shoppes isn't just a flea market—it's a community gathering place where nostalgia meets discovery. Located in the heart of Conway, Arkansas, we've created a space where local vendors and treasure hunters come together in a warm, welcoming environment.

Our carefully maintained booths showcase an eclectic mix of antiques, retro collectibles, thrift finds, and unique handcrafted items. Each vendor brings their own passion and expertise, creating a shopping experience that's as diverse as our community.

Whether you're hunting for vintage vinyl, searching for the perfect antique furniture piece, or browsing through stacks of classic books, you'll find it here—always at prices that respect your budget.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We're open Thursday through Sunday: Thursday-Saturday 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Sunday 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM. We're closed Monday through Wednesday to allow our vendors to restock and curate new items.

We'd love to have you! Simply click the "Become a Vendor" button or contact us directly at (501) 472-8277. We offer various booth sizes with flexible rental terms. All vendors must maintain clean, organized displays and provide quality merchandise.

Most of our vendors accept major credit cards, though some smaller booths may be cash-only. We recommend bringing some cash just in case, and there's an ATM available on-site for your convenience.

Many vendors are open to reasonable offers, especially for multiple items or higher-priced pieces. Don't be afraid to ask politely! However, please respect that some items are priced fairly and firm.

Yes! We have free parking available directly in front of the building. There's also additional parking in our rear lot accessible from the side street. Handicap accessible parking spaces are available close to the entrance.
